Upload Guide

1. How it Works

We are currently in Beta. To publish your game:

  1. Fill form: Enter your game details below.
  2. Submit: Click the "Submit Game" button.
  3. Review: Our moderators will check your game. Once approved, it will appear on the homepage.

2. Allowed Content

  • Web (HTML5): Must be hosted (e.g., GitHub Pages, Itch.io) or a single file.
  • Downloads: .zip, .exe, .apk (provide direct links).
  • External: Steam store pages, Itch.io links, etc.

3. Rules

  • ✖ No viruses, miners, or malware.
  • ✔ Keep thumbnails 16:9 for best results.
  • ✔ Keep game files under 100MB.
