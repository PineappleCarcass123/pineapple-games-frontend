About Us

Our Vision

To become the best platform for indie web games, creating a space where creativity knows no bounds and every game finds its player.

Our Mission

At Pineapple Games, our goal is to bring back the golden era of fun web games. We believe in an open platform where creators can share their work and players can jump straight into the action. We carefully curate our collection to ensure quality and safety for all our users.

Our Values

Community: We build for the players and the creators.
Creativity: We champion original ideas and bold designs.
Openness: We believe games should be accessible to everyone.
Quality: We strive for a seamless and enjoyable experience.
